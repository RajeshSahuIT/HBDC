Welcome to Hazaribag College of Dental Sciences & Hospital

Hazaribagh College of Dental Sciences & Hospital is situated in scenic locale of Hazaribagh city. The college was established in the year 2007 and is affiliated to the Vinoba Bhave University. It is the foremost dental college in Jharkhand was recognized by the Dental Council of India, New Delhi & Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India.

The college of dentistry is an exciting place to be, a place with unique history and tradition in our profession, yet eager to embrace the future.

The college is run and managed by SRINIVAS GROUP. Situated off NH 33, it is well linked to nearby town of Koderma, Ranchi, Gaya etc. with excellent road, train & air connectivity. The college is presently imparting courses in BDS with an intake of 100 students MDS (Masters in dental surgery) in various specialties
