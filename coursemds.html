Admission in MDS

  • A candidate for admission to the MDS course (Master of Dental Surgerw must have a recognized degree of BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery) awarded by Indian University In respect of recognize Dental College under section 10 (2) of the Dentists Act, 1948 or an equivalent qualillcation recognized by the Dental Council of India and should have obtained permanent registration with the State Dental Council. Candidate not possessing a recognized Dental qualification for the above purpose should secure the prior approval of hls qualification by the Dental Council of India before he can be admitted to the MDS Course of any University In India. The Candidate must have passed the BDS degree with DCI Registration from a recognized college. Candidate should qualify In MDS-NEET (National Ellglblllty Cum Entrance Test) of current year.

Affiliation

The B.D.S. and M.D.S. program at HCDSH are affiliated with Vinoba bhave University, Hazaribag. The Vinoba bhave University is the premier University of the State of Jharkhand and is in existence for nearly 24 years. The Vinoba Bhave University is the largest university in the state catering to the needs of higher education of more than two lakh students scattered over 75 colleges, 4 recognised institutions and 7 approved institutions. There are 21 Post Graduate University departments and 90 Post Graduate Centres.

Eligibility:

The candidate must have passed 10+2 or equivalent examination from a recognized Board, Council or University and must have obtained at least 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry and Biology taken together and must have passed in English. For candidates belonging to OBC minimum qualifying marks is 45% and SC/ST is minimum 40%. The candidate must attain the minimum age of 17 years at the time of admission.

Candidate should qualify in NEET (National Eliglbility Cum Entrance Test)of current year. Click here to check your result

Recognition

  • The B.D.S. Program at HCDSH is recognized by Dental Council of India
  • The M.D.S. Program at HCDSH is approved by Dental Council of India
