DEPARTMENT OF ORTHODONTICS

The Department of Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopedics is concerned with correction of malocclusion to normal function and esthetic relationship and to beautify facial form; growth modification; and prevention and interception of developing malocclusion. The department strives for

Academic excellence

  • Lectures
  • Seminars
  • Group discussion
  • Assignments,
  • Practical and laboratory work
  • Chair side discussion and demonstration
  • Journal Discussion and seminars by Postgraduates

Cutting edge research

  • Growth modification
  • Esthetics in orthodontics
  • Orthodontic implants

State of art diagnosis and treatment planning

  • CBCT
  • Digital cephalometry
  • Nemoceph software

Comprehensive Treatment

  • Fixed orthodontics with vast array of brackets : metal, ceramic, lingual, self- ligating
  • Invisible aligners
  • Removable orthodontics
  • Orthodontic implant
  • Orthopaedic treatment : growth modification with functional appliances
  • Prevention and interception of malocclusion in mixed dentition
  • Rehabilitation of patients with Cleft lip, Palate & Temperomandibular disorders
  • Interdisciplinary orthodontics with orthognatic surgery , accelerated orthodontics etc.

Ultramodern laborotary

  • Biostar
  • Hydro solder
  • Sandblaster
  • Digital wet and dry sterilization
