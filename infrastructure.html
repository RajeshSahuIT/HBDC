Campus

The college campus has a sprawling lush green lawns, lots of trees and plants with well planned and beautifully designed buildings divided into different blocks and wings. The college campus has all the amenities needing for high quality ofliving.

Classrooms

Fully furnished classrooms with attached Over Head Projector (OHD) and Slide Projector as teaching aids. All the classrooms are well ventilated & lighted.

Lecture Hall

State-of-the-art Air Conditioned Lecture Halls to fulfill the requirements of didactic teaching. The lecture halls are well illuminated, sound proof, wall to wall carpeted and self-contained in all respects. The lecture hall has an LCD projector, Over Head Projector (OHP), Computer System, state-of-the-art acaustics.

Students Library

Library facility is available for student to read various books of different departments.

Students Hostel

There are separate hostels for boys and girls. Each hostel has about 150 rooms. Rooms are on twin-sharing basis while Dormitories are on a triple sharing basis. All the rooms have attached baths and toilets. Rooms are provided with beds, mattresses, study tables, chairs, wooden inbuilt wardrobes and book racks

College Canteen

The college has an excellent canteen facility, comprising of a state-of-the-art kitchen, friendly catering staff and a pleasing environment. This canteen caters to the requirements of both the staff as well as the students. Good quality and tasty veg & non-veg foods are prepared and served under hygienic conditions.
